We’re Easily Fooled

Many have sounded the alarm, such as this guy, Dr Joseph Wiezenbaum of mit.

He wrote one of the first—maybe even the first—chatbot called eliza. Completely text-based, it was supposed to mirror what a Rogerian therapist would say.

It was so good that Dr Wiezenbaum argued with a person he worked with that it was not intelligent and was merely a trick. His colleague insisted it was intelligent.
