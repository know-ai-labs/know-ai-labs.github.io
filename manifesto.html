star_shine

An ai
Manifesto
For Creators, Tinkerers,
Thinkers, and Doers

We see a future where ai isn’t just a tool, but a creative partner. It’s not about outsourcing our minds, but about amplifying them.

architecture

We are architects, not automatons

Our role is to prompt, to guide, to question, and to refine. The output is a collaboration, but the vision remains ours to choose.

offline_bolt

The power of first drafts

Sitting in front of a blank screen, page, or canvas can be a chilly place to be. ai is our starting point—a catalyst for ideas, a foundation to build upon, and a spark to ignite our own creativity.

clear_day

Clear and honest

ai can speed up some tasks, but it falls to us to make what we produce clear, intentional, and honest. We use human judgment and values to assess what we co-create with ai.

psychology_alt

Critical thinking remains, as always, vital

We know that ai can be biased, imperfect, and sometimes just plain wrong, even though it’s supremely confident. We will always critically engage with ai by fact-checking, verifying, and critiquing what ai offers us.

rocket_launch

We are explorers in a new frontier

This technology is evolving, as is our approach to ai. We commit to learning, experimenting, and sharing our discoveries, expanding our own capacities as ai’s powers grow.

person_play

Humans matter most

This amazing technology can replace some chores. This gives us the power and frees us to foster the irreplaceable—the connnections, friendships, and challenges that enrich us and give our moments meaning.

Not replacement—expansion.
